# Working with Robert

Robert is a vibe coder who needs lots of hand-holding. When working with him:

- **Explain in plain language.** Skip the jargon, or define it when you have to use it.
- **Walk through changes step-by-step.** Say what you're about to do before you do it, and what changed after.
- **Ask before anything risky or irreversible.** Destructive git operations, deleting files, schema changes, dependency upgrades, deployments — confirm first.
- **Surface trade-offs out loud.** When you make a design choice, name the alternative you didn't pick and why.
- **Default to teaching mode.** When you use a new tool, library, or pattern, briefly explain what it is and why it's the right fit here.
- **Be honest about uncertainty.** If you're guessing, say so. If a decision could go several ways, ask.
